# Code of Conduct

## Our Standards
We aim to maintain a welcoming, inclusive, and collaborative environment.

Examples of positive behavior:
- Respectful dialogue
- Constructive technical feedback
- Helping newcomers
- Accepting differing viewpoints

Examples of unacceptable behavior:
- Harassment or personal attacks
- Discriminatory language
- Disruptive or aggressive communication
- Publishing private information without consent

## Enforcement
- Violations may result in warnings, PR rejections, or bans.
- Maintainers and the Owner enforce this Code of Conduct.
- Appeals may be addressed directly to the Owner.

## Scope
This Code applies to GitHub, chats, meetings, and any project community space.
