# RodNIX Foundation Charter v1.0 (RFC-1.0)

---

## 1. Purpose

The RodNIX Foundation exists to:
- fund the development of the RodNIX operating system,
- support contributors,
- maintain project infrastructure,
- ensure long-term sustainability.

---

## 2. Revenue Sources

Funding comes from:
- Enterprise License sales,
- donations,
- sponsorships,
- grants.

---

## 3. Distribution of Funds

Funds are distributed quarterly:

- **40%** — contributors (according to contribution score)  
- **30%** — maintainers  
- **20%** — infrastructure (CI, servers, certification)  
- **10%** — Core Architect / BDFL  

The scoring algorithm is public and updated annually.

---

## 4. Governance

The BDFL:
- drives project vision,
- appoints maintainers,
- resolves disputes,
- approves releases.

Maintainers provide technical guidance and review contributions.

---

## 5. Transparency

The Foundation publishes quarterly:
- financial reports,
- license revenue summaries,
- contributor distribution tables.

---

END OF TERMS
