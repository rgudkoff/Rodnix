# RodNIX Governance Model

RodNIX follows a BDFL (Benevolent Dictator For Life) governance system.

---

## Roles

### BDFL / Core Architect
- final authority on technical and organizational decisions,
- defines roadmap and architecture,
- appoints and removes maintainers.

### Maintainers
- review contributions,
- guide contributors,
- propose releases,
- maintain subsystems.

### Contributors
- anyone who submits patches or documentation,
- must sign the CLA.

---

## Decision Process

- Consensus is encouraged.
- If no consensus is reached, the BDFL decides.
- Releases require explicit BDFL approval.

---

## Conflict Resolution

Disputes are resolved by:
1. Discussion among contributors,
2. Maintainer review,
3. Final BDFL decision.
