# RodNIX Trademark and Naming Policy v1.0 (RTNP-1.0)

---

## 1. Ownership

The names:
- “RodNIX”
- “RodNIX OS”
- and all associated logos

are trademarks of the RodNIX Foundation.

---

## 2. Forbidden Uses

Without explicit written permission, you may not:
- name your fork or distribution “RodNIX”,
- use RodNIX logos in marketing,
- imply endorsement or affiliation,
- sell products under the RodNIX name.

---

## 3. Allowed Uses

You MAY:
- state factual compatibility (“Based on RodNIX”),
- use the name in academic/educational contexts,
- use screenshots in tutorials with attribution.

---

## 4. Enforcement

The Foundation may request renaming, rebranding, or removal of infringing
materials.

---

END OF TERMS
